How does it work

With a click of a button, you can find the credentials of your article.

Python Web Scraper

Through the use of a Python Web Scraper, Checkr finds the authors of the article, and will find information about the author. From the information, Checkr will validate whether the article is reliable.

Validity Score

Checkr will give you a score on the validity of your article. From 0, being the least valid, to 100, being extremely valid.
